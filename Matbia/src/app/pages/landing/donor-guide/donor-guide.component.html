A donor-advised fund (DAF) allows you to make a tax-deductible charitable donation but still retain the ability to advise how the funds will be distributed. The fund itself is a 501c3 charity and each donation has all the benefits of a charitable gift immediately, as soon as it is donated. Those funds are then kept available for the donor to advise where and when they should be allocated. Donations to the DAF are irrevocable and can only be distributed by grantmaking through the charity network.
