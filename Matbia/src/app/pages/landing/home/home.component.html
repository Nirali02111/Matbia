A BETTER WAY TO GIVE

Matbia is a innovative charity solution for the contemporary Jew. We’ve aptly fused Halachic ordinances with user-friendly technology to bring you a practical, in-depth analysis of where your charity is going.

It’s our hope that by using Matbia’s philanthropic tools, Jewish donors around the globe will better understand their finances, give within the guidelines of Halacha, and discover a manageable path to fulfilling this good deed.

THE TZEDAKAH CARD

THE TZEDAKAH CARD

DONATE DYNAMICALLY

Our sleek and unique charity card empowers users to make on-the-go giving decisions. With a simple tap of your card, direct a portion of your Maaser anywhere.

Donate your way by customizing the monetary amounts and digital functions for each of the card’s signature four corners.*

*Donations are made to Matbia Inc, a NJ Nonprofit corp. ("Matbia"). Matbia is a Donor Advised Fund, and once the donation is made, you only have the ability to advise Matbia as to which eligible organization to direct your donation. All donations are subject to the Matbia rules.

ADMIN PORTAL

ADMIN PORTAL

TRACK YOUR CHARITY

Our user portal offers a detailed analysis of all cards, accounts, transactions, and balances.

Donate to the charity of your choice with ease directly from your dashboard, set up recurring donations and monitor every payment in one convenient place.

tax deductible Donations

Matbia users obtain donation tax receipts for all funds in their account. Give confidently knowing that with Matbia you’re positioned to receive the best possible tax benefits for your charitable commitment.

4 corner feature

Matbia’s one-of-a-kind charity card’s four sides personalize the way you donate. Using its flexible features, set the amount and the way you give to work for you.

RECURRING DONATIONS

Keeping consistent with your contributions is easy with Matbia’s periodic payments. Schedule your donations with just a few clicks, and analyze all activity in our user portal.
