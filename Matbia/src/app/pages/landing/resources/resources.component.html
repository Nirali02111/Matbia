Frequently Asked Questions

Request a card by signing up at the following link : https://matbia.org/signup and we’ll mail one out to you.

Matbia is free for cardholders.

Matbia is a DAF (Donor-Advised Fund), which means that all funds held in Matbia are tax-exempt. You’ll receive a donation tax receipt via email for each amount transferred to Matbia.

You can link your bank account in one of two ways:
1.With your bank username & password.
2.With your routing & account numbers.
Once your account is linked, add funds at any time.

Use the Matbia portal to donate to any organization.
The card works with the Donary platform and Donary devices, including the Donary Mobile, Donary Pocket, event devices, phone system, and online donation page.
In the last few months we have worked with other charity platforms, credit card processors and gateways, to be able to accept Matbia cards.

If your bank is linked with a username and password, funds are available immediately.

If your bank is linked with routing and accounting numbers, it will take 2-5 business days.

Yes! In addition to making manual deposits into your account, you can set up auto-recurring transfers and a replenishment option when the account falls below a preset amount.

Once a donation is made to an organization, they’ll receive an email prompting them to log into Matbia’s online portal. There, they can link a bank account to redeem funds.

Matbia charges each organization within the range of 2.2% - 2.9% of all donations, depending on the volume of contributions. (There are no transaction fees or any other hidden charges.)
