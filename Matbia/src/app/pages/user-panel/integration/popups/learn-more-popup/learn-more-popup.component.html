Tutorial: integrate with account number

If you never used yet your selected processing merchant, then complete 'step 1' and you will receive an email notifying you about the newly opened account. Copy from the email your Matbia account # found on top and proceed to the next step.

If you already used a selected merchant, and it has not yet been integrated to your current account.

  • Check your emails if you ever received it and locate there your account number or;
  • Please contact customer service to get your account number created from your selected merchant.
